The Briefing

Three critical modules. Master them all to earn your Cyber-Scout designation and defend your digital territory.

MISSION_01 //

Metadata Mystery

Every photo hides a secret dossier — GPS coordinates, timestamps, device fingerprints. Learn to read the invisible trail left behind in digital images.

● EXIF · Geolocation · Digital Forensics
MISSION_02 //

URL Detective

Phishing links are poisoned traps disguised as friendly paths. Train your eye to spot spoofed domains, suspicious redirects, and deceptive anchor text before you click.

● Phishing · Domain Spoofing · Link Safety

Section 03 — Final Protocol

The Cyber-Tracker's Oath

// Digital Pledge of Honor //

I, a Scout of the Digital Wilderness, pledge to use technology as a force for good.

I will protect the privacy of others — never stealing, exposing, or misusing digital data.

I will stay vigilant against deception — questioning links, sources, and suspicious signals.

I will share knowledge, not exploit it — teaching fellow scouts to navigate the digital terrain safely.

On my honor as a Taguig Scout, I promise to be a guardian of the grid — prepared, responsible, and always helpful.
